Welcome to Konchamar

Where Luxury Meets the Pacific

Nestled along the pristine shores of La Libertad, Konchamar offers an unparalleled escape from the ordinary. Our boutique resort combines world-class amenities with the natural beauty of Playa El Majahual, creating an exclusive sanctuary for discerning travelers.

With limited accommodations ensuring privacy and personalized service, every stay at Konchamar is crafted to perfection. From sunrise surf sessions to sunset cocktails, discover the art of coastal living.
